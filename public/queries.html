When is it?

Hack Dat will take place from December 9th at 6:00pm and last until December 11th at 6:00pm. The judging event will take place starting at 6:30 on the 11th at Launchpad as well (click for judging event information)

Where will it be held?

The actual event will be held wherever you are doing your work; in other words, there is no central location that you are required to be at for the duration of the contest. All we care about it that you show up for the judging event at Launchpad at 6:00 on the 11th with an app in hand!

Can non-participants attend the judging event?

Of course, actually we're hoping you will stop by and take a look at the final applications made by your own local developers.
